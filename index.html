Trial Session — Everything You Need to Know

The most important step before your wedding makeup.

What happens in a trial?

We discuss your outfit, jewellery, venue, and create 1-2 look options on your face. You can take photos and decide.

How long does it take?

60-90 minutes for a complete trial look including hairstyling.

Cost

₹1,500 per trial session. This amount is fully adjustable in your final booking amount.

When should I book a trial?

Ideally 2-4 weeks before the event. Earlier is better for peak wedding season.

Should I bring anything?

Yes! Bring your outfit or a photo, jewellery, and any reference images you've saved.

What if I don't like the look?

We'll modify it on the spot. Your satisfaction is non-negotiable — that's the whole point of a trial.

Your Safety Matters

Hygiene & Safety Practices

We take hygiene as seriously as we take beauty. Here's exactly what we do for every single client.

Fresh Disposables Every Time

New sponges, mascara wands, lip applicators, cotton pads, and earbuds for every client. Nothing is reused — ever.

Sanitized Brushes

All brushes are washed with brush shampoo and sanitized with Dettol antiseptic solution after every single use.

Hand Hygiene

Hands are washed with soap and sanitized before touching any product or client's face. Gloves available on request.

No Double-Dipping

Products are scraped onto a palette first — never applied directly from the container to the face.

Authentic Products Only

No expired products, no grey-market items, no duplicates. All purchased from authorized retailers only.

Clean Kit & Setup

The makeup kit is cleaned and organized before every assignment. Sanitized workspace, clean towels, and tidy setup guaranteed.

3. Travel Charges

Travel charges are communicated upfront before booking confirmation. They are non-negotiable and separate from the makeup package price. Early morning surcharges (before 5 AM: ₹300) apply as informed.

The Mohul commits to arriving 30 minutes before the scheduled time. However, delays due to unavoidable circumstances (traffic, weather, etc.) will be communicated immediately. No refund is applicable for delays under 30 minutes.

The Mohul reserves the right to use photos from the session for portfolio, social media, and marketing purposes. Clients may opt out by informing in writing before the session.
