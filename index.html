About Us

Our Story, Our Purpose

From a vision to make quality hygiene accessible, to a growing manufacturing enterprise serving India.

Company Overview

Frezool Industries Private Limited was established with a singular mission: to manufacture and deliver hygiene and home care products that Indian families and institutions can trust completely. As a private limited company registered under the Companies Act, 2013, we operate with full corporate governance, regulatory compliance, and professional management.

Our operations encompass the complete manufacturing value chain — from raw material sourcing and formulation development to production, quality testing, and distribution logistics. We are not just a factory; we are a systems-driven organization built for reliability and scale.

Headquartered in India, Frezool Industries serves a growing network of distributors, institutional buyers, and brand partners across multiple states, with ambitions to become a recognized name in the Indian FMCG manufacturing landscape.

Our Vision

To become one of India's most trusted and respected FMCG manufacturing companies, known for uncompromising quality, regulatory excellence, and meaningful contribution to the hygiene and home care sector.

Our Mission

To manufacture and distribute high-quality hygiene and home care products through ethical business practices, robust quality systems, and sustainable operations — while creating value for our partners, employees, and stakeholders.

Our Journey

The Founding Story

Frezool Industries was born from a simple observation: millions of Indian households deserve access to hygiene products that are not just affordable, but truly effective and safe. The gap between premium imported brands and unorganized local manufacturers presented an opportunity — to build a professional, systems-driven manufacturing company that could bridge this gap.

With deep domain knowledge in chemical formulation and a commitment to regulatory compliance, the founders set out to create an organization that would earn trust not through marketing noise, but through manufacturing excellence and corporate transparency.

From setting up the first production line with stringent quality protocols, to launching the Phenora brand as a showcase of our capabilities, every step has been guided by the principle of doing things right — even when no one is watching.

Sustainability

Responsible Manufacturing

Our commitment to the environment, our people, and future generations.

Environmental Responsibility

At Frezool Industries, we recognize that manufacturing has an environmental footprint — and we take that responsibility seriously. Our sustainability approach is practical, measurable, and integrated into our daily operations rather than being a mere marketing statement.

Safe Chemical Usage

We prioritize formulations that use chemicals approved by relevant Indian regulatory bodies. Raw materials are sourced from certified suppliers with proper MSDS documentation. We avoid restricted substances and continuously explore bio-based alternatives.

Waste Management

Our waste management practices include segregation of hazardous and non-hazardous waste, proper disposal through authorized agencies, and efforts to minimize production waste through process optimization and recycling where feasible.

Water & Energy

We monitor water consumption and effluent discharge as per PCB norms. Energy-efficient machinery and optimized production scheduling help reduce our carbon footprint per unit of output.

Sustainability Goals

We are working towards measurable sustainability targets including reduced packaging waste, increased use of recyclable materials, and achieving zero liquid discharge capability in the medium term.
